VICbot and custom signatures

Hi old friend. I just wanted to let you know that when you do nominations at COM:VIC, you have to replace your custom signature with the standard signature in the nominator field (I've done it for your latest nomination [3]). For some reason, VICbot won't process promoted nominations if the nominator uses a custom signature. What I do is create the nomination and then copy and paste over my custom signature from User:INeverCry/Stuff. It's a slight pain in the ass, but otherwise the bot just ignores the promoted nomination. I hope to see plenty more VIC nominations from you going forward. Have a good one. lNeverCry 21:20, 16 January 2017 (UTC)
